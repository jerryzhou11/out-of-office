Out of Office by Bethany Cho and Jerry Zhou....or....The Dead Joe-ts' Society

You're the newest nepo baby CEO, and your secretary has gone missing. Go downstairs, and search each floor for a way down until you can get to the bottom of this (literally). Find her before 5PM, or you'll have to try again tomorrow. Use WASD to move around, and left click to swat away your pesky employees. And don't forget your morning coffee!
